Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is a highly rated dish on allrecipes.com and comes with the promise of being delicious with crispy edges and a chewy middles. This top-rated recipe has over 14,000 rave reviews and has only been on the website since August of last year.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • baking soda
  • water
  • salt
  • all-purpose flour
  • chocolate chips
  • walnuts optional

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
