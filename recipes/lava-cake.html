Lava Cake

Description

The following recipe has gotten a gleaming review of "bes lava cake I've ever had!" and is meant to serve four. This is a molten cake recipe best topped with berries or vanilla ice cream

Ingredients

  • chocolate
  • butter
  • eggs
  • sugar
  • all purpose flour
  • cocoa powder
  • sale

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter together using a double boiler.
  2. Beat the eggs and sugar together, then add the chocolate and remaining ingredients.
  3. Bake until the sides are set but the center is jiggly.
